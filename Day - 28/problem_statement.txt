1.Convert a Binary Tree to Doubly Linked List In-Order

Given a binary tree, convert it to a doubly linked list using in-order traversal. The left pointer of a tree node acts as the previous node, and the right acts as the next.

Input :

      10
     /  \
    5   15


Output (DLL):

5 ⇄ 10 ⇄ 15
