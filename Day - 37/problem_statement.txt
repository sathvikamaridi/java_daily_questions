Maximum Sum of Two Non-Overlapping Subarrays

You are given an integer array nums and two integers L and M.Find the maximum sum of two non-overlapping subarrays with lengths L and M.

Test Case 01 :

nums = [0,6,5,2,2,5,1,9,4]
L = 1
M = 2
Output: 20

Test Case 02 :
	
nums = [3,8,1,3,2,1,8,9,0]
L = 3
M = 2
Output: 29
