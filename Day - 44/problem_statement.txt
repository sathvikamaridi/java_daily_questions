Find All Subarrays with Given Sum

Given an array of integers and a target sum K, print all subarrays that sum to K.

Test Case 1:

Input: [1,2,3,4,2], K = 6 

Output: [1,2,3], [2,4]

Test Case 2 :

Input: [3,4,7,2,-3,1,4,2], K = 7 

Output: [3,4], [7], [7,2,-3,1], [1,4,2]
